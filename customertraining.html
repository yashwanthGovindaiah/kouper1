Customer Training by KOUPER Lubricants ( Seminars & Technical training for Customers and Our Distributors )

At KOUPER Lubricants, we believe that knowledge is power. To help our customers get the most out of our high-performance lubricants, we offer comprehensive training programs designed to enhance your team's understanding and proficiency in lubrication management. Our training services are tailored to meet the specific needs of your organization, ensuring that your staff is equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to maximize the efficiency and longevity of your equipment.
We the team in Kouper always strives to share our ideas, educate and assist our customers through Technical Seminars, Articles, case studies, application manuals and latest insights in lubrication and Tribology.

Learning is an Endless process! Whereas Research and Innovation is out of Curiosity!

Our Customer Training Programs Include:

Fundamentals of Lubrication

Understanding the basics is crucial. Our introductory courses cover essential topics such as lubricant types, properties, and functions. Participants will gain a solid foundation in lubrication principles, helping them make informed decisions about lubricant selection and application.

Advanced Lubrication Techniques

For those looking to deepen their expertise, we offer advanced training on lubrication best practices, including precision lubrication, contamination control, and advanced application methods. These courses are designed to improve equipment reliability and performance.

Condition Monitoring and Analysis

Learn how to effectively monitor the condition of your lubricants and equipment. Our training covers oil analysis, wear debris analysis, and other diagnostic techniques that help you identify potential issues before they lead to costly downtime.

Lubrication Management Systems

Implementing a robust lubrication management system is key to maintaining equipment health. Our training programs teach participants how to develop, implement, and maintain effective lubrication management systems tailored to their operational needs.

Custom Training Programs

We understand that each organization has unique requirements. Our custom training solutions are designed to address your specific challenges and goals. We work with you to develop a training program that fits your operational environment and objectives.

Hands-On Workshops

Practical experience is invaluable. Our hands-on workshops provide participants with the opportunity to apply their knowledge in real-world scenarios. These interactive sessions cover topics such as proper lubrication techniques, equipment inspections, and troubleshooting.

Safety and Compliance

Safety is our top priority. Our training programs include essential information on safe handling, storage, and application of lubricants, as well as compliance with industry regulations and standards (MSDS Training).
