About Us

Welcome to Kouper Lubricants!

At Kouper Lubricants, we are committed to delivering high-performance lubrication solutions tailored to meet the demands of various industries. With decades of expertise and innovation, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the lubricants industry, recognized for our quality, reliability, and customer-centric approach.

KOUPER LUBRICANTS PRIVATE LIMITED began its operation in 2017. The Company is located in Bangalore, South India, and is one of the leading producers of specialty lubricants in the region.

The facility has a production capacity of 2800TPA (Tones per annum).

We are into manufacturing of Specialty Lubricants, Grease and Industrial Cleaners, catering to multiple manufacturing industries across Indian Market.

At Kouper Lubricants, we offer redefined tribological solutions for various industries like Metal Cutting, Metal Forming, Textile , Cement ,Paper Automotive, Construction & Mining

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the efficiency and longevity of machinery and vehicles through advanced lubrication technology. We strive to provide our customers with superior products that ensure optimal performance, reduce downtime, and promote sustainability.

  • Innovation and Excellence: Continuously developing advanced lubricants through cutting-edge technology and rigorous testing to meet and exceed industry standards.
  • Customer Focus: Delivering exceptional products and services tailored to the unique needs of our customers, ensuring reliability and satisfaction.
  • Sustainability: Promoting environmentally responsible practices in our production processes and products to contribute to a sustainable future.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Kouper Lubricants leads the way in the Indian lubricants market, setting benchmarks for quality and innovation. We aim to be the preferred partner for businesses seeking dependable lubrication solutions by

  • Positioning ourselves as one of the leading specialty lubricant manufactures in India.
  • Keep investing in Technology, Systems and Resources whilst uploading high ethical values, Sustainability and Business excellence which we constantly strive for.

Our Core Values

Core values are the principles and priorities that guide an organization's actions. They represent the foundational commitments and deeply held beliefs that allow a company to navigate complex situations while keeping their identity and culture at the forefront.
