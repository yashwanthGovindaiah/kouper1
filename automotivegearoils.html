KOUPER PROMAX GEAR Oils

Kouper Promax Multi-Grade Gear Oils are engineered to provide superior protection and performance for a wide range of gear applications. Utilizing advanced additive technology and high-quality base oils, Promax gear oils ensure outstanding wear protection, thermal stability, and extended service life.

Key Features and Benefits:

• Superior Wear Protection
• Thermal Stability
• Extended Service Life
• Excellent Load-Carrying Capacity
• Smooth Operation

Applications:

• Suitable for use in a variety of gear systems, including those found in heavy-duty industrial, automotive, and marine applications.
• Ideal for gearboxes, differentials, and other gear-driven equipment requiring high-performance lubrication.

Performance Standards:

• Kouper Promax Multi-Grade Gear Oils meet or exceed the stringent requirements of major industry standards and specifications, ensuring reliability and peace of mind.

  • KOUPER PROMAX SAE 20, 30, 40, 50, 90 and 140 Oils – SAE MINERAL OILS

    KOUPER PROMAX SAE mineral oils are a range of straight grade minerals oil designed for use where a mono-grade oil is required.

    The modern iteration of the classic monograde engine oil, KOUPER PROMAX SAE mineral oils, is tailored to precisely meet the demands of diverse climate conditions. Facilitating smoother start-ups, especially for vehicles in prolonged storage, these oils can also serve as effective break-in oil for optimal engine performance.
