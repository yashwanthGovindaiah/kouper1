COMPRESSOR OIL - PROKOMP SERIES

Our wide range of compressor oils are formulated with mineral, semisynthetic & fully synthetic base stocks for rotary screw, rotary vane, centrifugal, reciprocating compressors & vacuum pumps.
Kouper PROKOMP series formulated with unique blend that meets all the demands of industrial compressors. These thermally stable fluids with a specially selected additive combination to ensure long service life in the industrial air compressors.
PROKOMP series help in keep parts clean with low varnish, sludge and deposits, helping minimize maintenance and energy costs.
