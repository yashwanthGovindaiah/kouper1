NEAT CUTTING OIL - PROKUT SERIES

Our wide range of neat cutting oil finds its application in moderate and heavy-duty operations while providing superior machining performance with less consumption. The oils are applied generously to the more tool chip to get the maximum cooling effect, increase the tool life, and improve the productivity. These oils are ideal for Gear Hobbing, Thread Cutting, Drilling, Deep Drilling, Honing, Tapping, Turning, and Other demanding applications. Kouper PROKUT neat cutting oils are available in wide range like Synthetic, semi synthetic, and Mineral oil-based chemistries including Poly Alkylene Glycols, Poly alpha olefins, Esters and GTL based.
