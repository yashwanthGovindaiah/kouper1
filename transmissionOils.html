Kouper PROMAX Transmission Oils

Kouper PROMAX Transmission Oils are engineered to provide superior protection and performance for a wide range of transmission systems. Our advanced formulations are designed to meet the rigorous demands of modern vehicles and heavy-duty equipment, ensuring smooth and reliable operation even under the most challenging conditions.

• Exceptional Wear Protection
• Thermal Stability
• Enhanced Friction Control
• Oxidation Resistance
• Corrosion Protection
• Compatibility

Applications:

• Automatic Transmissions: Ideal for use in modern automatic transmission systems, providing smooth and responsive shifting.
• Manual Transmissions: Suitable for manual transmissions requiring high-performance lubricants for optimal gear protection and operation.
• Heavy-Duty Equipment: Perfect for heavy-duty on- and off-highway vehicles, including trucks, buses, and construction machinery.
• Specialized Systems: Meets the specific requirements of advanced transmission systems, including those with electronic controls.

Performance Standards:

Kouper PROMAX Transmission Oils meet or exceed the stringent requirements of major industry standards and OEM specifications, ensuring reliable and consistent performance across a wide range of applications.

  • 1. KOUPER PROMAX UTF – TRANSMISSION FLUID

    PROMAX UTF is high performance, multipurpose all weather transmission fluid. It’s a SAE 10W-30 fluid designed for use in transmissions, hydraulic systems, oil immersed brakes and other ancillary systems fitted to agriculture tractors, farm equipment’s & other offroad equipment’s.
    It has the benefit of being able to offer “one oil” for the hydraulic system, transmission, final drive and differentials where a “wet” brake system is employed

  • OUPER PROMAX ATF 3 – SYNTHETIC TRANSMISSION OIL FOR AUTOMATIC GEAR BOXES

    KOUPER PROMAX ATF 3 is crafted from high viscosity index paraffinic base oils and thoughtfully chosen additives like viscosity index improvers, antioxidants, and defoamers. This product delivers a harmonious blend that ensures smooth and controlled friction/wear characteristics.
    Its distinctive red colour not only gives it a unique appearance but also serves the practical purpose of facilitating easy detection of any potential leakage.
