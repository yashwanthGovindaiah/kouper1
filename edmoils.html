EDM OILS- PROEDM SERIES

KOUPER PROEDM series Electric Discharge Machining Oils are universal oils for all discharge machines especially for roughing, finishing and superfinishing work. Products are formulated with synthetic base oils & additives.

These oils have high flash point with less volatility helps to promote a safe & pleasant work environment. PROEDM series are low viscous oils also exhibit good circulation through spark gaps & fast removal of metal spurs.

  • KOUPER PROEDM 3
    PRODUCT KOUPER PROEDM 3
    K. Viscosity at 40 °C [mm2/s] 3
    Flash point [°C] ≥ 120
    Application
    • Electrical discharge machining in tool and die shops
    • Grinding fluid in glass grinding operations Extrusion Processes

  • Extended safety as it having high flash point
