INDUSTREAL CLEANERS - PROKLEAN SERIES

KOUPER LUBRICANTS providing outstanding cleaning chemicals. Our PROKLEAN series include both aqueous cleaners and solvent based cleaners that are manufactured to meet the demanding applications of our clients and their industries. In addition, we offer custom formulated cleaners because every industry is unique & every application is in different.

WE believe in “A CLEAN place is a SAFEEST place to work”

KOUPER offers a full line of environmentally sensitive aqueous cleaners and solvent based cleaners. Unlike many other cleaners PROKOOL series works at low temperatures, i.e., from Room temperature to 50°C. However, the final degreasing temperature depends on the type of oil and dirt.

This will benefit to our customers with high energy savings and helps in marching towards low carbon footprints.

The result is cutting-edge formulations with long lifetimes and high stability.

  • KOUPER PROKLEAN 70 SL – SOLVENT CLEANER

    KOUPER PROKLEAN 70 SL is a solvent-based cleaning agent with excellent dissolving effect. Removes all types oil, grease contamination's, wax and oil carbon. It can be used to clean tools, work pieces, motors, floors. Suitable for both manual and automatic cleaning application. Provides short time rust protection.
