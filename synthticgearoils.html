SYNTHTIC GEAR OILS- PROGEAR SERIES

Kouper PROGEAR SHC & PG Polyalphaolefin and Polyglycol based synthetic of gear oils. These are effective in application because of its extended oil drains intervals, subsequently reduce the waste and maintenance costs, heavy load carrying capacity and excellent performance in challenging operating conditions. Our enhanced formulation with performance additives of extreme pressure, anti-wear and friction modifiers can balance all the key properties including wet brake performance.

Our Kouper range of gear oils provide an innovative solution by

• Improving efficiency
• Reducing the overall cost of ownership
• High load carrying capacity
• Very good thermal and oxidation stability
• Micro pitting Protection
• Wear and friction Performance

  • KOUPER PROSYNTH SHC 68, 90….1000 (KOUPER PROSYNTH SHC Series - FULLY SYNTHETIC GEAR OIL (CLP HC))

    KOUPER PROSYNTH SHC series is an exceptional industrial gear oils designed to provide outstanding protection of gears and bearings, extended oil life even under extreme conditions. These synthetic base fluids that have exceptional oxidation and thermal properties and excellent low temperature fluidity. These oils are with high viscosity index properties, which can deliver less change in viscosity with changes in temperature, enabling wider operating temperature range and improved low temperature startup also better retention time on the gear.
