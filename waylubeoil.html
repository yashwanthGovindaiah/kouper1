LUBRICATION OILS - PROLUBE SERIES

KOUPER offers PROLUBE range of lubrication oils for machine tool slides and ways, these manufactured from premium base oils fortified with extreme pressure, tackiness, and friction modifying additives. This well-balanced blend provides superior slip-stick protection to ensure smooth and consistent movement for optimum surface finish. Furthermore, PROLUBE exhibits excellent separation from coolants. This property allows easy removal and reduces susceptibility to attack by bacteria growth in coolant sumps.
The product can also be used in oil circulation systems and for the lubrication of conveyor belts Plain bearings and guideways in the steel industry
